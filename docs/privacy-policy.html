Privacy Policy

App: xDrip4iOS
Last updated: April 2026

Overview

xDrip4iOS is an open-source continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) app. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data We Do Not Collect

xDrip4iOS does not:

  • Collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers
  • Create user accounts or require registration
  • Track your location or usage analytics
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Display advertisements

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All glucose readings, sensor data, and settings are stored exclusively on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly configure an optional integration:

  • Nightscout — If you configure a Nightscout URL, glucose data is uploaded to your own Nightscout server. We have no access to this server.
  • Apple Health — If enabled, glucose readings are written to Apple Health on your device. This is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Bluetooth

The app uses Bluetooth to communicate directly with your CGM sensor or transmitter. No Bluetooth data is sent to any server.

Notifications

Local notifications (glucose alerts) are generated on-device. No notification content is transmitted externally.

Open Source

xDrip4iOS is open source. You can review the full source code at github.com/JohanDegraeve/xdripswift.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
